    Concrete Coring Service

    MHS Group, specializes in providing advanced chemical anchor services designed to meet the needs of residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Using high-performance resin-based adhesive systems, we anchor steel studs, bolts, and other fastening elements into masonry and concrete surfaces to create robust, high-strength fixing points. These chemical anchors are ideal for heavy-duty applications, offering superior bonding strength compared to traditional mechanical anchors. The chemical curing process ensures that the base material remains undamaged, making it perfect for close-to-edge applications, low-quality concrete, and sensitive environments. We use Hilti’s innovative technology, renowned for its quality and precision, and are equipped to handle even the most demanding anchoring projects. Chemical anchors provide the ideal solution for situations requiring a high load-bearing capacity while ensuring no stress on the base material. At MHS Group, we ensure that our chemical anchoring services offer long-lasting, secure, and reliable results for all types of construction and structural needs.
